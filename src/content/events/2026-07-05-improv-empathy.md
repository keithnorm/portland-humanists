First Sunday, from FH. Improv is a practice in connection, joy, support and empathy. Kristen Schier is an actor, improviser, teacher, and clown who has extensive improv teaching, directing, and performing experience. She holds a BFA in Theater Arts from, and taught at, the University of the Arts, and has studied improv at Second City, Magnet Theater, and Annoyance.  Kristen has toured the country teaching and performing at various festivals, is the former Co-Artistic Director of Curious Comedy in Portland Oregon, and former Improv Producer at the Philly Improv Theater. (First Sundays start with 1/2 hour of live music, offer childcare, and may last until noon.)
