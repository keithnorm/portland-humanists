Dr. Lisa Martinez examines the spread of scientific misinformation online and discusses effective strategies for countering false claims while promoting critical thinking.

This timely presentation addresses:
- How misinformation spreads on social media
- Psychological factors behind belief in false claims
- Effective vs. ineffective debunking strategies
- Building scientific literacy in communities
- The role of trusted messengers
- Case studies: vaccines, climate change, and public health

Dr. Martinez draws on research in psychology, communication studies, and public health to offer practical guidance for anyone concerned about the erosion of trust in science and evidence-based policy.
