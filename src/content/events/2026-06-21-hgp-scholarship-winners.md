From FH & Zoom. Amanda Feller introduces the winners of the 2026 HGP Del Allen Memorial Scholarship. The two topic prompts were: Advanced AI and its impacts on Humanism's 10 Commitments; or the Separation of Church & State in the public K-12 system. The Committee adjudicated 17 high school essays from across Metro Portland, selecting three: Drishti Singh, “More than Algorithms"; Krrish Ravuri, “Duty in the Age of Artificial Intelligence”; and Brooks Colclazier, “A Philosophical Analysis of the Role of Humanism in a Future with Advanced AI”. The winners have already begun their applied professional studies in AI which will continue throughout their undergraduate careers.
