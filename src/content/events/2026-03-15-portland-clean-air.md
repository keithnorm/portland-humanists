At Friendly House. Greg Bourget, Executive Director and Lead Researcher of the non-profit Portland Clean Air, explains how the non-profit measures air quality variation within the Portland area using the world's most advanced mobile monitors: the MiniWRAS diesel particulate monitor, and the Tiger XT VOC monitor (purchased through HGP-raised funds). Bourget finds Portland industries impacting human health and what can be done about them, and will update us on ending corporate donations to politicians in Oregon, which is a very hot topic as the Oregon legislature decides to scrap or keep their word about Senate bill 4204.
