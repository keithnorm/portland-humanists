From FH. The People's Depot (TPD), a program of Ground Score Association, is a unique bottle redemption program underneath the Morrison Bridge Eastside. From humble beginnings during covid, TPD is now influencing the Oregon bottle bill. Hear from Kristofer Brown, who manages day to day operations at the depot, on how TPD is different, who uses our depot services, and why this work is crucial in todays modern landscape. Kristofer Brown is TPD's manager after spending almost 5 years on the streets of Portland collecting bottles and cans. (1st Sundays start with 1/2 hour of music, and offer childcare.)
