Over Zoom. Border walls are spreading around the world, including right here in the US. Sky Island Alliance has studied the impacts of border barriers on wildlife movements since 2020. Join Eamon Harrity to learn about these monitoring efforts, how different species respond to border barriers, and the options that exist to improve connectivity for wildlife. Eamon is the wildlife program manager at Sky Island Alliance, a conservation nonprofit based in Tucson, Arizona. Eamon completed his Bachelor's degree at the University of Vermont and his Master's degree at the University of Idaho.
