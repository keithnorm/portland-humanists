Your support helps the Humanists of Greater Portland further humanist values in the community, fund our annual scholarship, and maintain our educational and outreach programs.

All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

## How to Donate

**By mail:**

Send a check payable to *Humanists of Greater Portland* to:

PO Box 8475
Portland, OR 97207

## Membership

Becoming a member is also a meaningful way to support HGP — all membership contributions are tax-deductible donations. See our [Join page](/join) for membership levels and the online application.

## Questions?

Email us at [info@portlandhumanists.org](mailto:info@portlandhumanists.org) with any questions about donations or membership.
