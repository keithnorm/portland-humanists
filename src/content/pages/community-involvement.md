The Humanists of Greater Portland supports local nonprofits that reflect our humanist values — organizations working for the common good, human dignity, and a sustainable community.

## Organizations We Support

HGP has supported organizations including:

- **Oregon Public Broadcasting** — independent, nonprofit public media
- **Transition Projects** — services for people experiencing homelessness
- **Sisters of the Road** — community café and advocacy for people in poverty
- **SOLV** — environmental cleanup and stewardship across Oregon
- **Sunshine Division** — food and clothing assistance for Portland families in need

## Get Involved

Members are encouraged to volunteer with these and similar organizations. If you know of a nonprofit that aligns with humanist principles, bring it to our attention at [info@portlandhumanists.org](mailto:info@portlandhumanists.org).

Bring food or clothing donations to our Sunday programs — we collect regularly for local organizations serving people in need.
