Sunday programs are held at **Friendly House Community Center**, 1737 NW 26th Ave, Portland, OR 97210.

Programs run from **9:45 AM to 11:30 AM** every Sunday. Coffee and refreshments are available, with socializing after the program.

## Getting There

Friendly House is located in the Northwest neighborhood of Portland, near NW 26th and Thurman. Street parking is available on NW 26th Ave and surrounding streets.

## Zoom Option

Can't attend in person? Programs are also broadcast via Zoom. Check the individual event listing for the Zoom link, or contact us at [info@portlandhumanists.org](mailto:info@portlandhumanists.org).

## Photography & Recording

Programs are often recorded and posted on our [YouTube channel](/events). A camera is set up in the room. If you prefer not to be filmed, please sit in the back rows away from the main camera angle.

## Accessibility

The meeting room at Friendly House is accessible. Contact us at [info@portlandhumanists.org](mailto:info@portlandhumanists.org) if you have specific accessibility needs or questions.
