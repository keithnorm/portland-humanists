The Humanist Division of Celebrants offers secular alternatives for life's most important ceremonies and moments of reflection — the humanist equivalent of traditional clergy.

## Officiate Your Ceremony

Our humanist celebrants are legally ordained to officiate:

- **Weddings & commitment ceremonies** — personalized, secular ceremonies that reflect your values and tell your story
- **Memorial services & celebrations of life** — meaningful gatherings that honor a life without religious framing
- **Coming-of-age ceremonies** — secular alternatives to religious rites of passage

## Philosophical Counseling

Our counselors are available to discuss life's important questions from a humanist perspective — whether you're facing a major transition, wrestling with an ethical dilemma, processing grief, or simply exploring the big questions of existence.

## Connect With Us

To connect with a humanist celebrant or counselor in the greater Portland area, email [info@portlandhumanists.org](mailto:info@portlandhumanists.org).
