The Humanists of Greater Portland is committed to serving the broader community — not just our own members. Our outreach programs reflect core humanist values: compassion, community, and a commitment to improving human well-being.

## Our Outreach Programs

**[Annual Scholarship Contest](/scholarship)** — We award an annual scholarship to recognize students in the greater Portland area who demonstrate humanist values through academic achievement and community involvement.

**[Community Involvement](/community-involvement)** — HGP supports local nonprofits aligned with humanist values, from environmental organizations to services for people experiencing homelessness.

**[Humanist Celebrants & Counselors](/celebrants-counselors)** — Our ordained humanist celebrants officiate weddings, memorial services, and other ceremonies, and offer philosophical counseling as a secular alternative to clergy.
